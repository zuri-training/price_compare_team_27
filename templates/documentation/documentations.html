How to

Search for products

When you come on Pricify to find a specific product, the best thing to do is to use the search feature which you will find at the top of our start page. There you can search for phones and specific models to easily find what you are looking for.

When you enter something into the search box, you will be shown a list of suggestions related to the words you just entered. The suggestion can be great in helping to narrow down your search and more easily find what you are looking for.

if you for example, are on the look out for an iphone and you enter this in the search bar, you will get a list of different iphones, you can then choose to click any of the shown options
