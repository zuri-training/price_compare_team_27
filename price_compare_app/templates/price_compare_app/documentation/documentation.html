Getting Started

Introduction

Pricify is a platform that compares prices of different brands of phones across multiple platforms. Pricify, a price comparison platform, aims to simplify users search when trying to find a mobile device they Would like to buy by providing them with prices of phones based on their search parameters from various ecommerce websites in Nigeria like Jumia and Konga and also provide products ratings and reviews for users to see and make intelligent and smart purchasing decisions quicker and easier.

The platform has a streamlined focus towards mobile phones from various top brands like Samsung, Apple, Infinix, Tecno, Oppo, Xiaomi and a host of others
