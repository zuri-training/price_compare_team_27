How to

Compare Prices

The “Compare Prices” feature allows you to match price of products across multiple e-shops. With this feature, you can discover the price of any device. However, to compare prices, you'll have to be a registered user. Here, to compare prices

Simply search for a device or select a mobile device from the categories or wishlist. Once a product is selected, you'll have an option to compare prices. Upon click, you'll be redirected to the price comparison pagewhere you can see the price tags of selected device.
