How to

Sign up

By signing up you can register and create a Pricify account to enjoy its amazing features and offers. Creating a Pricify account is free and easy. To sign up, follow these steps below:

  1. Click on the "Sign Up" Button on the top right corner of the header. You'll be redirected to a sign up page requesting your information

  2. Input your correct information on the fields provided and click on the "Sign Up" button

  3. Once you click on the sign up button, you've successfully created your account and you'll be redirected to your dashboard; and you can now start exploring our price comparison page and other features.
